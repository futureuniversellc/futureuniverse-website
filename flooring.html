Our Flooring Division

Premium Flooring for Every Space

Future Universe's Flooring Solutions division is Oman's trusted provider of high-quality, durable flooring systems for commercial, industrial, and residential spaces. From gleaming epoxy floors to elegant stone installations, we transform surfaces into statements.

Our expert installers use only premium-grade materials and cutting-edge application techniques, ensuring your floors look exceptional and withstand the test of time.

Flooring Types

Our Flooring Solutions

Premium flooring systems for every environment — from corporate headquarters to industrial warehouses.

Epoxy Flooring

High-gloss, seamless epoxy floor systems ideal for commercial and industrial spaces. Extremely durable, chemical resistant, and easy to clean.

Vinyl Flooring

Premium vinyl planks and tiles offering the look of wood or stone with superior durability and waterproof performance for any room.

Industrial Flooring

Heavy-duty flooring systems designed for warehouses, factories, and logistics centers — built to withstand heavy machinery and forklift traffic.

Tile Flooring

Precision tile installation in ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone for residential, commercial, and outdoor applications.

Stone Flooring

Marble, granite, limestone, and travertine flooring installation with expert cutting, fitting, and polishing for a truly luxurious finish.

Maintenance Services

Comprehensive floor maintenance, polishing, recoating, and repair services to keep your floors looking brand new for years.
